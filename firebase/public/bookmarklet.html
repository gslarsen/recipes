How to Use

1
Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible. In Chrome: View → Always Show Bookmarks Bar or press ⌘+Shift+B
2
Drag the "Save to Pam's" button above to your bookmarks bar
3
Navigate to any recipe page on the web
4
Click the bookmarklet - the recipe will be saved to your collection!
Note: You must be signed in to Pam's Recipe Collection for this to work. The bookmarklet will prompt you to sign in if needed.
